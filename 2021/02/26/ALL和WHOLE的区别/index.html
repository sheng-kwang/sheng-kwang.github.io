The difference between All & Whole

  1. 定冠词的位置(after all, before whole)
    All the
    The whole
  2. all强调个体，whole强调整体
    all: everthing, one, etc.
    whole: complete, packet, group, container
  3. 不可数名词用all, whole常于可数名词单数一起用
    all the money, all the air (正确)
    the whole money, the whole air (错误)

一些例句：

The baby cried all the time
                         the whole time

all the time（always; cried, stop, cried, stop）:
—+++—++—-+++—-+++—++>
the whole time（non-stop）:
++++++++++++++++++++++++>


I studied all time day
              the whole day

All my friends came.
The whole group of my friends came.

All cities were destroyed.
所有的城市都被摧毁了
Whole cities were destroyed.
有些城市整个被摧毁了

All students need to come to class early tomorrow.
The whole class needs to come early tomorrow.
